Welcome, I'm Dr. Jane Doe

BSc, MSc, HCPC Registered Psychologist

Life can be overwhelming, and sometimes we need a guiding hand. My passion is to create a warm, non-judgmental space where you feel safe to explore your thoughts and feelings. Based in the heart of the Midlands, I offer therapy that is tailored to your unique needs and goals.

My approach is integrative, drawing on evidence-based practices like Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) and Mindfulness. Together, we can work to understand patterns, develop coping strategies, and build a more fulfilling, resilient life.

How I Can Help

I offer support for a range of challenges, providing both online and in-person sessions.

Individual Therapy

One-to-one sessions to work through personal difficulties, from anxiety and depression to life transitions.

Couples Counselling

Support for partners to improve communication, resolve conflicts, and strengthen their connection.

Stress & Burnout

Develop practical strategies to manage stress, prevent burnout, and find a healthier work-life balance.
